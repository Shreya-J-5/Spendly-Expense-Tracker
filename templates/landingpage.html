About Spendly

Spendly is a modern, intuitive expense tracker designed for everyone. Whether you're a student managing your pocket money, a professional tracking your finances, or a family coordinating household expenses, Spendly makes financial management simple and enjoyable.

Our mission is to empower individuals with the tools and insights they need to take control of their financial lives. We believe that smart money management should be accessible, engaging, and straightforward.

Why Choose Spendly?

Fast & Intuitive

Add expenses in seconds with our streamlined interface. Quick entry, zero complexity.

Secure & Private

Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We respect your privacy above all.

Smart Analytics

Get clear insights into your spending patterns with beautiful visualizations.

Dark Mode UI

Easy on the eyes with our modern dark interface design. Built for comfort.

Category Management

Organize expenses by categories and track income sources effortlessly.

Mobile Friendly

Access your finances anywhere, anytime on any device. Fully responsive design.

Built With Modern Technology

Spendly is built using Flask (Python backend), SQLite database, and Bootstrap frontend. This ensures reliability, speed, and a seamless user experience. Perfect for students, professionals, and anyone serious about managing their finances.
