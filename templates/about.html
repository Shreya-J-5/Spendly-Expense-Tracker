About Spendly

A modern, intuitive expense tracker designed for everyone. Whether you're a student managing your pocket money or a professional tracking your finances, Spendly makes it easy.

Our Mission: To empower individuals with the tools and insights they need to take control of their financial lives. We believe that smart money management should be simple, accessible, and engaging.

Our Vision

Why Spendly?

Spendly was created with a simple vision: make financial management accessible to everyone. Too many expense tracking apps are complicated, feature-heavy, and confusing. We decided to do things differently.

Built for You

Whether you're:

  • A student learning about personal finance
  • A professional managing multiple income streams
  • A family tracking household expenses
  • Anyone looking for financial clarity

Spendly is designed with you in mind. Our clean interface, intuitive controls, and powerful insights make managing money not just easy, but enjoyable.

Built with modern web technologies: Python Flask backend, SQLite database, and Bootstrap frontend. This ensures reliability, speed, and a great user experience.
